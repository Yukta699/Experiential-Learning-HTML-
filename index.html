About Us

The Computer Science Department is dedicated to providing quality education in the field of computing and modern technology. We aim to build strong technical knowledge and practical skills among students.

Our curriculum covers core subjects such as Programming, Data Structures, Database Management Systems, Operating Systems, and Computer Networks, along with emerging areas like Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, and Data Science.

The department encourages innovation, research, and hands-on learning through projects, workshops, and seminars. Students are motivated to participate in technical events and develop real-world problem-solving abilities.

With experienced faculty members and a supportive learning environment, we focus on overall development, teamwork, and preparing students for successful careers in the IT industry.

Our vision is to create skilled professionals who can contribute to technological growth and make a positive impact on society.
